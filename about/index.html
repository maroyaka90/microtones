About

Microtones is an electronic music event series and label that is curated by otodojo and organized by a crew of over 15 volunteers. The events take place in the historic Forest City Lodge #180, home to the Elks for over 100 years.

Together with the members of the Elks, we have created an inclusive space for appreciating music from musical artists from around Ithaca and beyond who are pushing the creative boundaries of electronic music. Partnering with this historic organization, Microtones regularly offers support toward the physical upkeep of the Lodge and contributes to an Elks goal of sponsoring activities focused on community building. The Microtones events showcase the historic Elks Lodge of Ithaca and their mission, while promoting an atmosphere of inclusion and tolerance.

We have hosted over 30 events with over 70 artists from around the world spanning the far reaches of electronic music.
