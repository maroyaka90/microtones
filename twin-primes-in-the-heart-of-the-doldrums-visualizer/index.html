Twin Primes - In the Heart of the Doldrums [visualizer]

Twin Primes’ track, “In the Heart of the Doldrums” brings you through a somber tumultuous journey, leaving you in an air of uncertainty, yet the color healing vignettes laid on top of the Painted Hills of Oregon from Andrew Charles Edman help to ease you through this emotion.

Music by Twin Primes

Visualizer by ACE

This is a part of wave~seed, a compilation in support of those working for a just and equitable society.

All proceeds go to Alliance of Families for Justice, Multicultural Resource Center, and Southside Community Center.
